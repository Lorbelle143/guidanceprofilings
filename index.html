What We Offer

Our Services

The Guidance & Counseling Office provides a range of support services for every NBSC student

Peer Counseling

One-on-one and group counseling sessions facilitated by trained peer counselors to help students navigate personal, academic, and social challenges.

Academic Guidance

Support for students experiencing academic difficulties — study strategies, course concerns, and connecting students with the right resources.

Career Guidance

Career exploration, aptitude assessments, and guidance to help students make informed decisions about their future career paths.

Personal & Social Support

A safe, confidential space for students dealing with personal or social concerns — relationships, family issues, stress, and emotional well-being.

Appointment Scheduling

Book a counseling session with our guidance counselors. Walk-ins are welcome during office hours — Mon to Fri, 8AM to 5PM.

Group Activities & Programs

Workshops, seminars, and team-building activities organized by the League of Peer Counselors to promote holistic student development.
