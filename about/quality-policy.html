Quality Policy Statement

Our commitment to excellence in education and service

"
At Northern Bukidnon State College, we commit to provide gender-responsive and high-quality standards of educational programs and service delivery that fosters diversity, equity, and inclusivity and cultivates the holistic development of lifelong learners to meet the evolving needs of the 21st-century workforce. We commit to full adherence to applicable and regulatory requirements and continuous improvement in all our administrative and academic operations to satisfy our clients, stakeholders, and the community. We commit that this policy is communicated, implemented, maintained, and reviewed periodically so that clients satisfaction and quality services are at its best.
