Tips

Student Wellness Tips

Get enough sleep

Aim for 7–9 hours. Sleep affects mood, focus, and overall mental health.

Stay active

Even a 20-minute walk can reduce stress and boost your mood significantly.

Stay connected

Talk to friends, family, or a counselor. You don't have to face challenges alone.

Take breaks

Study in intervals. Short breaks improve retention and prevent burnout.

Eat well

A balanced diet supports brain function and helps regulate emotions.

Ask for help

Reaching out is a sign of strength. The GCO is always here for you.
